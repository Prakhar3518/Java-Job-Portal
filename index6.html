Job Title: AI Engineer

Company: Tesla

Location: Various locations, including Palo Alto, CA, and remote options

Company Overview:

Tesla, Inc. is an American multinational automotive and clean energy company that specializes in electric vehicles, battery energy storage, and solar energy solutions. At Tesla, our mission is to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy. With groundbreaking innovations in AI and autonomous driving, Tesla offers a dynamic and fast-paced work environment where creativity and problem-solving skills thrive.

Job Description:

We are looking for a motivated and talented AI Engineer to join our team at Tesla. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning, along with hands-on experience in using TensorFlow. You will play a critical role in developing and deploying AI solutions that power Tesla's advanced technologies, including autonomous driving and manufacturing processes. This is a full-time position with opportunities for growth and innovation within the company.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement AI algorithms for autonomous driving and computer vision applications.
  • Develop and optimize deep learning models using TensorFlow for various AI use cases.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including hardware engineers and data scientists, to integrate AI solutions into Tesla's products.
  • Apply computer vision techniques for object detection, image segmentation, and classification.
  • Analyze and interpret complex data to drive AI model improvements and scalability.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in AI, deep learning, and computer vision technologies.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science, Engineering, or related field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Minimum CGPA of 8.0 or higher.
  • Proficiency in Deep Learning and Computer Vision algorithms.
  • Hands-on experience with TensorFlow for building and deploying AI models.
  • Strong foundation in Machine Learning concepts and techniques.
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills with a keen eye for detail.
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience with other deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch.
  • Knowledge of autonomous driving technologies and sensor fusion.
  • Experience with data visualization and data processing tools like NumPy and Pandas.
  • Familiarity with cloud-based AI services such as AWS SageMaker or GCP AI Platform.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
  • Generous paid time off and parental leave.
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company match.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and career advancement.
  • Employee discounts on Tesla vehicles and energy products.
  • Dynamic work environment that encourages innovation and creativity.

How to Apply:

To apply for this position, please visit Tesla’s career page at tesla.com/careers and search for the "AI Engineer" role. Alternatively, you can submit your resume and cover letter directly through the provided application link.
