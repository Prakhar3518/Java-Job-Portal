Job Title: DevOps Engineer (Senior)

Company: Netflix

Location:

"Netflix's primary headquarters is located in Los Gatos, California, with major offices in Los Angeles, New York, and Amsterdam, serving as hubs for innovation and collaboration. While many roles are based in these locations, Netflix offers flexible remote work options for certain positions. This flexibility allows employees to contribute to our global mission from anywhere, ensuring a balance between work and life."

Company Overview:

Netflix, Inc. is the world's leading streaming entertainment service with over 200 million paid memberships in over 190 countries enjoying TV series, documentaries, and feature films across a wide variety of genres and languages. At Netflix, we strive to entertain the world, and our teams are dedicated to providing the best streaming experience through continuous innovation and technological excellence.

Job Description:

We are looking for an experienced and highly skilled DevOps Engineer to join our team at Netflix. As a Senior DevOps Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and managing infrastructure to support Netflix's large-scale, distributed systems. The ideal candidate has expertise in AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes, and can thrive in a remote work environment. This is a full-time, remote role with opportunities for impactful contributions to Netflix's continuous delivery and operational excellence.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design, build, and maintain scalable and secure cloud infrastructure using AWS.
  • Manage containerized applications using Docker and orchestration with Kubernetes.
  • Automate deployment processes to ensure seamless integration and delivery pipelines.
  • Collaborate with development and operations teams to improve system reliability and performance.
  • Implement monitoring and logging solutions to ensure system stability and availability.
  • Work remotely while coordinating effectively with global team members across different time zones.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Minimum CGPA of 10.0 or equivalent.
  • Extensive experience with AWS services, including EC2, S3, Lambda, and RDS.
  • Proficiency in Docker for containerization and Kubernetes for container orchestration.
  • Strong understanding of CI/CD pipelines and automation tools.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills with a focus on operational efficiency and automation.
  • Ability to work independently and manage priorities in a remote work environment.

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience with infrastructure as code (IaC) tools such as Terraform or CloudFormation.
  • Knowledge of scripting languages like Python or Bash for automation.
  • Experience with monitoring tools like Prometheus and Grafana.
  • Familiarity with security best practices in cloud environments.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
  • Unlimited paid time off to maintain work-life balance.
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company match.
  • Access to cutting-edge technology and professional development resources.
  • Remote work flexibility with home office setup support.
  • Employee discounts on Netflix services.
