Job Title: Data Scientist (Junior)

Company: Meta

Location: Hybrid (On-site & Remote)

Company Overview:

Meta Platforms, Inc. (formerly known as Facebook) is a leader in social networking and technology innovation, focused on connecting people and building communities. Meta's mission is to give people the power to build community and bring the world closer together through its various apps and services, including Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Oculus. At Meta, we strive to create an inclusive, diverse, and collaborative environment where employees can grow and make a positive impact.

Job Description:

We are seeking a passionate and results-driven Data Scientist for a junior position at Meta. The successful candidate will have a solid foundation in Python, Pandas, and Deep Machine Learning. You will work on analyzing complex datasets, developing machine learning models, and deriving actionable insights to support Meta's various products and services. This is a full-time position offering a hybrid work environment, with both on-site and remote work opportunities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Analyze large and complex datasets to derive actionable insights.
  • Develop and deploy deep learning models to improve user experiences and product functionality.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including engineering, product, and research to solve challenging data problems.
  • Utilize Python and Pandas for data manipulation, analysis, and visualization.
  • Work in a hybrid setup, balancing remote work with in-person collaboration.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Mathematics, or related field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Minimum CGPA of 7.0 or higher.
  • Proficiency in Python with 1+ years of experience.
  • Experience with Pandas for data analysis and manipulation.
  • Strong understanding of Deep Machine Learning concepts and frameworks.
  • Ability to work effectively in a hybrid work model, with strong self-management skills for remote work.
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience with deep learning frameworks such as TensorFlow or PyTorch.
  • Knowledge of data visualization libraries like Matplotlib or Seaborn.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms such as AWS, GCP, or Azure.
  • Understanding of big data technologies like Apache Spark.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
  • Generous paid time off and parental leave.
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company match.
  • Professional development opportunities and access to Meta’s learning resources.
  • Hybrid work setup with flexibility for remote work.
  • Collaborative work environment with opportunities for growth.
