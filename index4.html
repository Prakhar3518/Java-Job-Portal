Job Title: Backend Developer

Company: Google

Location: Various locations, including Mountain View, CA, and remote options

Company Overview:

Google LLC is an American multinational technology company specializing in Internet-related services and products, which include online advertising technologies, search engine, cloud computing, software, and hardware. Known for its innovation and cutting-edge technology, Google’s mission is to organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful. As a leader in AI and cloud computing, Google provides a collaborative and inclusive work environment that promotes career growth and development.

Job Description:

We are looking for a skilled Backend Developer to join our dynamic team at Google. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in Python and Django, with extensive experience in building and maintaining RESTful APIs. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to develop scalable backend solutions that power various Google products and services. If you are passionate about backend technologies and enjoy solving complex problems in a fast-paced environment, we would love to hear from you.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, and maintain scalable backend services using Python and Django.
  • Build and maintain RESTful APIs to support mobile and web applications.
  • Collaborate with frontend developers, product managers, and other stakeholders to design and implement new features.
  • Optimize existing systems for scalability, reliability, and performance.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and testable code.
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to a collaborative team environment.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies to drive innovation.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Strong proficiency in Python with at least 3+ years of experience.
  • Hands-on experience with Django framework for building scalable web applications.
  • Expertise in RESTful API design and development.
  • Solid understanding of database technologies such as SQL and NoSQL databases.
  • Experience with cloud platforms such as Google Cloud Platform (GCP) is a plus.
  • Familiarity with version control systems like Git.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and a proactive attitude.
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work effectively in a team environment.

Preferred Skills:

  • Experience with containerization and orchestration tools like Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Knowledge of microservices architecture.
  • Understanding of frontend frameworks like React or Angular is a plus.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
  • Generous paid time off and parental leave.
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company match.
  • Access to Google’s learning resources and career development programs.
  • Opportunities for growth and internal mobility.
  • Work in a dynamic and diverse environment with some of the brightest minds in the tech industry.

How to Apply:

To apply for this position, please visit Google’s career page at careers.google.com and search for the "Backend Developer" role. Alternatively, you can submit your resume and cover letter directly through the provided application link.
