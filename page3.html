Requirements to Apply:

To join our team as a Front-End Developer, we are looking for individuals who meet the following criteria:

  • A strong academic background with a minimum CGPA of 8 or equivalent.
  • No history of backlogs throughout your academic journey, demonstrating consistency and dedication to excellence.
  • Expertise in developing iOS applications using SwiftUI for building user-friendly and responsive interfaces.
  • Proficiency with UIKit to create visually appealing and intuitive UI components, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • Hands-on experience with Core Data for efficient data management and persistence in iOS applications.

Desired Skills:

We are looking for candidates who bring a mix of technical expertise and a passion for user-centered design:

  • Exceptional problem-solving skills, with the ability to debug complex issues and optimize app performance.
  • Experience with integrating RESTful APIs and working closely with backend teams to deliver seamless app functionality.
  • Solid understanding of version control systems like Git, with experience in collaborative development workflows.
  • Strong attention to detail, ensuring every aspect of the user interface is pixel-perfect and aligned with Apple’s high design standards.
  • A passion for staying up-to-date with the latest trends in iOS development, design patterns, and best practices.

Package Offering:

  • Entry-Level: $90,000 - $110,000 per year, with additional benefits like stock options, health insurance, and performance bonuses.
  • Mid-Level: $120,000 - $140,000 per year, including enhanced stock options, comprehensive health coverage, and wellness programs.
  • Senior-Level: $150,000 - $180,000 per year, with premium benefits, significant stock grants, and opportunities for career advancement.

Important Dates:

The application process includes an online test, which will be conducted on November 15, 2024. Be sure to apply early and prepare for the exam to showcase your skills and knowledge in iOS development.
